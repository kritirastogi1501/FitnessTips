GOOD HEALTH FITNESS TIPS

Based on UN SDG - 3

  • Exercise
  • Sleep
  • Mental Health
    

EXERCISE REGULARLY

Do 30 minutes of moderate-intensity exercise on most days of the week.

  • Cardio: Running, cycling, swimming,water aerobics,riding a bike,dancing
  • Strength Training: Weightlifting, bodyweight exercises,pilates,working with resistance bands.
  • Flexibility: Yoga, stretching,Forward Lunges,Side Lunges

GET ADEQUATE SLEEP

Aim for 7-9 hours of sleep each night to allow your body to recover and rejuvenate.

  • Establish a sleep routine: Go to bed and wake up at the same time every day
  • Create a restful environment: Keep your bedroom dark, quiet, and cool
  • Limit Naps: Avoid long naps during the day, especially in the afternoon
  • Avoid screens before bed: Limit exposure to blue light from phones and computers
  • Watch Your Diet: Never eat large meals or drink a lot of fluids late at night

FOCUS ON MENTAL HEALTH

Mental well-being is crucial for overall health. Take time to relax and de-stress.

  • Meditation: Practice mindfulness and deep breathing exercises
  • Hobbies: Engage in activities you enjoy
  • Social Connections: Spend time with family and friends
  • Limit Screen Time: Reduce the amount of time spent on screens
  • Set Realistic Goals: Break tasks into smaller, manageable steps and set achievable goals
  • Practice Gratitude: Keep a gratitude journal or take time each day to reflect on positive aspects of life
